    Create your perfect home

EB Homes is a local licensed contractor with decades of experience in home remodeling and building single family residences, specializing also in handicap accessibility projects. With a proven track record on projects large and small, EB Homes approaches every project with the highest level of ethical and professional standards and integrity, with a personal touch. Whether you need a room addition, a kitchen or bath remodel, or wider doors and ramps for wheelchair access and a handicap-accessible ADA approved shower, we are the builder dedicated to earning your trust.
